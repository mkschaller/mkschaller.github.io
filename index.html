Who is McKenna?

McKenna discovered web design in the mid-2000s, but it became her passion in college. She loves simple, minimalist design and pretty typography. Both are reflected in her web design style. Most recently, she has ventured into programming to learn JavaScript. She wants to apply her new skill towards a future career in front-end web development. McKenna is always challenging herself to be her very best and applies hard work and motivation towards all that she does.

Currently, she is studying for an Associate in Graphic Arts and an Associate in Web Design and Development at McHenry County College.
